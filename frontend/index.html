Meteor Madness 🚀

An interactive asteroid impact simulator powered by NASA & USGS data.
Explore orbits, predict impacts, and test strategies to defend Earth.

Launch Simulator

🛰️ The Problem

NASA tracks thousands of Near-Earth Objects. Current tools focus on detection and orbital tracking, but they lack accessible impact simulations for policymakers and the public. Our challenge: make asteroid risk visualization interactive, accurate, and understandable.

✨ Features

🔭

3D Orbit Viewer

Visualize asteroid trajectories using Three.js.

🌍

Impact Heatmap

Map craters, seismic waves, and tsunami risks with Leaflet.

📊

Impact Analysis

Calculate energy, crater size, seismic magnitude, and tsunami height.

🛰️

Mitigation

Experiment with kinetic impactors and gravity tractors.
